The Holy Grail —
4 Steps from Mark-Up to Exit

The complete strategy in a single overview — mark up your charts, wait for the break, act, and stay until the safety line breaks.

⏱ 12 min 🟢 Beginner 🎯 Strategy

This Is the Entire System

After understanding how to draw trend lines and how to do top-down analysis, everything comes together in the strategy. And the strategy is beautifully simple. It has four steps — and only four steps. Every trade you ever take in this system will follow these exact steps.

"This right here, essentially, is the strategy in an absolute literal nutshell in one singular illustration. Drawing lines and watching price either respect or break them."

The Four-Step Strategy

1

Mark Up the Charts

Do your full top-down analysis. Draw all trend lines. Make sure nothing is intersecting.

2

Wait for the Break

Sit on your hands. Watch price. Wait for it to break ONE of your trend lines. Don't predict — just wait.

3

Take Action

When price breaks a line, enter the trade. The broken line becomes your action line. The other becomes your safety line.

4

Stay Until Safety Breaks

Stay in the trade as long as price respects the safety line. Exit when price breaks it. Simple.

Step 1 — Mark Up Your Charts

This is where you spend most of your time. Before any trade, before even thinking about an entry, you must complete your top-down analysis. Draw all your upward and downward trend lines from the monthly time frame down to your trading time frame.

At this stage, your lines are just lines. They don't have names yet. You don't know which one price will break. That's fine — and that's intentional. You're preparing, not predicting.

Step 2 — Wait. Just Wait.

This is where most traders fail — not because they don't understand the strategy, but because they can't sit still. The strategy requires patience. After you've done your analysis, you do nothing until price breaks one of your lines.

⏳

Patience Is the Edge

We never know which line price will break first. Will it break the downward trend line (bullish)? Or the upward trend line (bearish)? We don't guess. We don't predict. We literally sit and wait for price to tell us. The market will always show you the answer — you just have to be patient enough to receive it.

Step 3 — Take Action (The Entry)

When price breaks through one of your trend lines, that is your trigger. You now have permission to enter a trade. The line that was broken becomes your action line — it triggered your decision. The opposing trend line becomes your safety line — it protects you.

✅

The Break IS the Signal

You are not entering based on a gut feeling, a news event, or a pattern you think you see. You are entering because price did something objective and measurable: it crossed your trend line. There is no ambiguity. Price broke the line → you enter the trade.

Step 4 — Stay Until the Safety Line Breaks (The Exit)

Once you're in a trade, one question matters: has price broken the safety line? As long as the answer is no, you stay in the trade. The moment the safety line breaks, you exit. No exceptions.

This removes all emotion from the exit decision. You don't try to "feel" when the trade is ending. You don't exit early because you're nervous. You don't hold too long hoping for more. The safety line tells you. Period.

🛡️

The Safety Line Is Everything

The safety line tells you how long to stay in the trade. It equals your stop-loss, your risk management, and your exit signal — all in one. As long as price is respecting it, you stay. The second it breaks, you're out.

It Works on ANY Market

Any Instrument. Any Time Frame. Any Market.

Because the strategy is purely price action — drawing lines on a chart — it doesn't matter what you're trading. If you showed a chart to a veteran trader without labeling it, they couldn't tell you the instrument, the time frame, or the market. But they could instantly see where the lines should go and where the entries are. That's the power of this system.
